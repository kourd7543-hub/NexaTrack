Interactive Map

Visual representation of your real-time location

How Interactive Map Works

NexaTrack uses Leaflet.js with OpenStreetMap and ESRI Satellite layers to visually display your real-time location on a dynamic, interactive map.

  • Live animated marker positioning
  • Switch between Normal and Satellite view
  • Zoom in/out functionality
  • Reverse geocoding shows your address automatically
  • Popup with full address and accuracy info
