About NexaTrack

Your smart and reliable real-time location tracking solution.

What is NexaTrack?

NexaTrack is a modern web-based application that allows users to track their real-time geographical location instantly. It uses advanced browser Geolocation technology to provide accurate coordinates and display them on an interactive map. Location history is saved locally, and devices can be shared or recovered using unique codes or phone numbers.
