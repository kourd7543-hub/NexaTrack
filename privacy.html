Privacy Focused

Our Privacy Commitment

NexaTrack is designed with privacy-first principles. We do not store, collect, or share any user location data on any server. All processing happens entirely within your browser.

  • No server-side storage of any kind
  • Location data stays in your device's browser (localStorage)
  • Permission-based access — browser always asks before sharing location
  • Share links use Google Maps directly, no third-party tracking
  • You can clear all history any time from the dashboard
